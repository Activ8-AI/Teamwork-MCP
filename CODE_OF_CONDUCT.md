Code of Conduct
===============

We are committed to a welcoming and inclusive community.

Expected Behavior
-----------------

- Be respectful, considerate, and collaborative
- Accept constructive feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the community

Unacceptable Behavior
---------------------

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, or political attacks

Reporting
---------

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open a confidential issue or contact a maintainer.
